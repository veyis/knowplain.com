When you are accumulating wealth, a 7% average return is a 7% average return, regardless of what order those returns happen in. But once you start withdrawing money, the order of those returns becomes critical.

This is called **Sequence of Returns Risk**. In this video, we visualize exactly how a market crash early in your retirement can devastate your portfolio, even if the market recovers later.
