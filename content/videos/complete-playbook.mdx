Welcome to the Complete Retirement Playbook. In this video, we'll walk through the foundational concepts of retirement planning, focusing on the math rather than just picking a date on a calendar.

## The core philosophy

The traditional way to think about retirement is: "I'll retire at 65." But that's fundamentally backward. Retirement is a financial state, not an age. You reach it when your assets can generate enough income to cover your expenses indefinitely, factoring in inflation and market volatility.
