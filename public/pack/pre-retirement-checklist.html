Pre-Retirement Checklist

Know Plain · Educational only · Use in the 5–10 years before leaving full-time work

Accounts & visibility

  • ☐ List every account (401k, IRA, Roth, taxable, HSA, pension)
  • ☐ Track them in one dashboard (Empower works well)
  • ☐ Know the expense ratio of each major fund

Income & spending

  • ☐ Track 3 months of real spending
  • ☐ Separate needs vs wants for a retirement budget draft
  • ☐ Estimate Social Security at ssa.gov retirement estimator

Withdrawal readiness

  • ☐ Decide a starting withdrawal rate band (e.g. 3–4%) — not a guarantee
  • ☐ Plan a cash / short-bond buffer for early retirement years
  • ☐ Know which account you’d draw from first — confirm with a tax pro

Risk & sequence

  • ☐ Mentally rehearse a 30–40% market drop in year 1 of retirement
  • ☐ Write your “bad year” rule
  • ☐ Avoid increasing stock risk right after a crash to “catch up”

Admin

  • ☐ Beneficiaries updated
  • ☐ Estate docs / POA location known
  • ☐ RMD awareness if applicable (IRS RMD FAQs)
